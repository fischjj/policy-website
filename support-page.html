Answers to Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Lusit App.
  2. Go to the login screen and tap on "Forgot Password".
  3. Enter your email address and tap "Submit".
  4. Check your email for a password reset link and follow the instructions.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team via email. See the contact information below.

How can I delete my account?

To delete your account, please navigate to settings in the app, then edit profile and delete account.

What data does Lusit App collect?

Lusit App collects data to provide a better user experience. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy
